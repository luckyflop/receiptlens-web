Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

At Receipt Lens AI, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we handle your data when you use our Chrome Extension.

1. Data Collection

We only collect the minimum information necessary to provide our services:

  • Email Address: Used solely for authentication and to manage your scan limits/license status via Google Login.
  • Images: When you upload a receipt, the image is sent to Google Vision API for temporary processing. We do not store your images on our servers.

2. Data Usage

Your data is used to:

  • Verify your trial or pro license status.
  • Extract text and financial information from your receipts.
  • Perform real-time currency conversion based on your selected target currency.

3. Third-Party Services

To provide high-quality service, we integrate with the following trusted providers:

  • Google Cloud Vision: For Optical Character Recognition (OCR).
  • Google Firebase/Firestore: For managing user scan counts securely.
  • Lemon Squeezy: For secure payment processing and license management. We never see or store your credit card details.
  • Exchange Rate API: To provide accurate, real-time currency conversion rates.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Your receipt data is processed in real-time and is not permanently stored or sold to any third parties.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated scan count data at any time by contacting our support team.
