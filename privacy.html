Privacy Policy for Receipt Lens AI

Last Updated: February 2026

Receipt Lens AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use our Chrome extension.

1. Data Collection

Receipt Lens AI is designed to process receipt data locally and via secure AI APIs. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties.

  • Receipt Images: Scanned images are processed to extract data and are not stored on our permanent servers unless explicitly synced by the user.
  • Identity: We use Google OAuth (identity permission) only to authenticate your account and sync your history across devices.

2. Use of Permissions

  • activeTab: Used only to interact with the receipt document currently open in your browser.
  • storage: Used to locally cache your preferences and scanned history for a faster experience.

3. Third-Party Services

Payments are securely handled by Lemon Squeezy. We do not see or store your credit card information. Our AI processing may use secure cloud-based models, but your data is never used to train global models without consent.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.
